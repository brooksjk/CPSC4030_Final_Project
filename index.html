Navigating Road Safety: A Data-Driven Exploration of Motor Vehicle Collisions in NYC

New York City is America's most populous city. It is the bustling city that never sleeps. However, due to the number of people who live and work in the city, traffic and thus car accidents have become a concern to many. To understand the crash data that the city of New York provides better, it is essential to visualize the data to come to proper conclusions, to educate, and to make decisions on possible ways to fix this issue in NYC.
